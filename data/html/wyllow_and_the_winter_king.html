Wyllow and the Winter King

The foxglove and frost motif symbolizes the forbidden love between Wyllow (a Seelie Archdruid) and the Winter King (of Unseelie blood). Each clue leads the party to the intersection of Seelie and Unseelie realms, where winter and spring collide in harmony and discord. The party will uncover the tragic, fractured love story that lies beneath the surface of Wyllow's curse.

But there is more to the story than what meets the eye: while the key can free Wyllow from his trance, it is only by destroying Halaster that the Winter King can be restored to his rightful self.

However, as the locket's 🔓 power is revealed, Wyllow will share the bitter truth that goes beyond his own suffering. The curse 🔮 may have been lifted, but the Winter King ❄️ remains in a state of perpetual slumber—an eternal victim of Halaster's meddling.
